# Control Matrix

All findings below are based on synthetic data (see `/data`). Priority reflects risk rating combined with the sensitivity of the affected population (e.g., admin/elevated access is treated as higher priority than standard access).

| Finding | Risk Rating | Evidence | Control Gap | Business Impact | Recommendation | Priority |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Terminated-user access exceptions (27) | High | `sql/01_access_control_tests.sql`, Test 1 | Deprovisioning control does not consistently remove access at termination | Unauthorized access risk; terminated users may retain system access or show post-termination login activity | Automate HR-to-IT deprovisioning trigger; weekly exception report | 1 |
| Privileged users without MFA (28, incl. 14 System Administrators) | High | `sql/01_access_control_tests.sql`, Test 2 | MFA is not enforced as a precondition for privileged access | Highest-value accounts (admin/System Administrator) protected by single-factor authentication only | Enforce MFA via conditional access policy before privileged access is active; weekly review | 1 |
| Admin/elevated access provisioned before approval (25) | High | `sql/01_access_control_tests.sql`, Test 4 | Approval control does not reliably gate provisioning for high-risk access | Elevated/admin access granted without a completed approval step | Prioritize admin/elevated requests for automated, system-enforced approval gating | 1 |
| Admin/elevated missing business justification (62) | High | `sql/01_access_control_tests.sql`, Test 6 | Documentation standard not enforced for high-risk access | Cannot demonstrate business need for admin/elevated access if reviewed | Require written justification and named business sponsor for all admin/elevated requests; monthly review | 1 |
| Access provisioned before approval (84, all levels) | Medium/High | `sql/01_access_control_tests.sql`, Test 3 | Approval control does not reliably gate provisioning | Access workflow control operates inconsistently across the broader population | Configure workflow tooling to technically block provisioning until approval is on record | 2 |
